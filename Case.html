Discovery and Planning The company starts by having
discussions with the client to understand their needs,
objectives, and target audience. They gather all relevant
information about the project's scope,
goals, and timeline. Once the requirements are clear,
the development and design team collaborates
to create a detailed project plan. This plan outlines
the project's milestones, deliverables,
technologies to be used, and the estimated timeline
for each phase of the project.

Despite their long-standing success in the retail industry,
SunaSport faced a critical challenge: they lacked an online presence.
The absence of a website and mobile app limited their ability to serve
their customers in the digital age, especially during the rise of e-commerce.
